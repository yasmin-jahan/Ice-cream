frequently asked questions

Does the museum serve savory food?

We've got your sweet tooth covered and can help coordinate your catering needs to satisfy any other cravings!

Can I arrange for external food to cater to my event?

Yes! With our museum buyout option, we can help you coordinate catering through our preferred vendor!

Would it be possible to have a private event in the afternoon/mid-day?

Anything is popsicle at Museum of Ice Cream! If you are interested in hosting an event during mid-day, submit an enquiry and one of our team members will reach out to dream something up.

How long do MOIC parties typically last?
  • Birthday parties and team building generally last 1-2 hours
  • Museum buyouts can last as long as your heart desires!
Do you have gluten free and dairy free options?

Yes we do! Please note that there is always a possibility of cross-contamination.

Do you offer gift bags?

We do! We can even scoop up a customized bag for you and your sprinkle crew.

Is there on-site parking available?
  • New York — There is no street parking available outside the museum, however there are plenty of parking garages within walking distance!
  • Austin — The Domain has complimentary parking. More parking lots are available behind the museum; as well as the nearby pink garage!
  • Chicago - There are available parking lots by SpotHero that are located within the vicinity of Museum of Ice Cream Chicago. We recommend that you reserve your parking slot in advance on SpotHero via the link here.
Can I bring a cake or cupcakes?

Private events, VIP table rentals, and museum buyouts can certainly bring cakes or other pastries (especially if you're celebrating a birthday!). We work with some great vendors who will be sure to make the celebration extra sweet.

Can I decorate the space for my party?

Decoration is allowed during private events and museum buyouts!

What if I would like to cancel my event or what if some of my guests are unable to attend my event?
  • If you have purchased Ticket Insurance ($4/ ticket) along with your General Admission tickets, you will be eligible for a refund. Otherwise, we can always reschedule the tickets at a later time.
  • Refunds are permissible for private events or buyouts with a 3 weeks notice. We can always reschedule the event as well!
What happens if more guests arrive than the contracted amount?

Should you wish to accommodate more guests than the contracted amount on the day itself, we will charge your card on file for the additional amount.
